# **App Name**: Feiticos Secretos do Amor

## Core Features:

- Spell Casting Request: Users can submit a request to have Lady Soraya cast a love spell on their behalf.
- Personalized Ritual Quiz: A brief quiz to personalize the spell casting ritual.
- Secure Form Completion: A secure form to collect necessary information for the spell casting.
- Spell Casting Confirmation: Confirmation message after the spell is cast, indicating it may work as soon as tonight
- Disclaimer and Terms: Display the terms of service, to ensure the spell is being cast with good intentions and cannot be undone.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep rose (#D81B60), inspired by the romantic essence of Lady Soraya's love spells, this vibrant color captures the energy of passion and love.
- Background color: Pale rose (#FCE4EC), creates a soft and dreamy backdrop, evoking a sense of femininity.
- Accent color: Fuchsia (#C2185B), a slightly brighter version of the primary color to guide the user's eyes to the essential buttons.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins' sans-serif for a clear and modern reading experience.
- Fully responsive layout to support different screen sizes.
- Use icons and images related to love and magic.
- Subtle animations such as pulsating glow effects, heartbeats etc. on key elements to capture the visitor's attention
